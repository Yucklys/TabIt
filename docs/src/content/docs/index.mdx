## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Local Processing" icon="laptop">
    All tab categorization runs locally in your browser using TF-IDF and Leiden clustering. No data leaves your device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Grouping" icon="puzzle">
    Leiden community detection clusters related tabs based on title and URL similarity, creating meaningful groups automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto Grouping" icon="rocket">
    New tabs are automatically sorted into existing groups or new ones as you browse, keeping your workspace organized in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customizable" icon="setting">
    Control group size, granularity, and grouping behavior. Rename groups, pick colors, and choose your preferred language.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
